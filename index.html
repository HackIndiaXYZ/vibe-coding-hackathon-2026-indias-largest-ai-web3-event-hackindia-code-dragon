Built by Ojas, a developer from Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh who codes at the intersection of AI systems and cinematic UI design.

REN × NATSUKI is a personal exploration of what it means to give an AI genuine character — not just intelligence, but emotional depth, memory, and presence.

The system is built from scratch with a full emotion engine, relationship tracker, memory system, and intent classifier — all hand-crafted in Python.
